# Vocabulary Preview


# Language Takeaway

1. **In a bit of a hurry**: 表示没有时间、正匆忙地移动
2. **Contact details**: 指名片上的信息，如电话、邮箱地址等，方便他人联系你
3. **Slipped my mind**: 形象地描述偶然忘记了某事

# Putting it Together

1. **Around here / Around the corner**: 用于描述地理位置。**Around here** 指在附近；**Around the corner** 则具体指在另一条街或拐角处。
2.  **Terrible with**: 这是一个非常实用的句型，用于表达“在某方面很糟糕”或“记性不好”。常见的用法包括 **terrible with numbers**（对数字不敏感）、**terrible with directions**（不识路/路痴）或 **terrible with technology**（不擅长电子产品）。

# Fluency Builder

1. **How’s it going?**: 视频强调这是一个比 "How are you?" 更自然、更地道的非正式问候方式
2. **It happens to me all the time**: 这是一句非常实用的社交客套话，用于缓解对方的尴尬

# Dialogue

**Socializing: Forgetting a Name (Dialogue Transcript)**

| Character | Dialogue                                                                                                                                                              |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Anna**  | Nick, **how’s it going**?                                                                                                                                             |
| **Nick**  | Oh hey, what are you doing in this neighborhood? Do you live **around here**?                                                                                         |
| **Anna**  | Actually, my office is right **around the corner**. It was great to meet you last week at the conference. I really enjoyed our conversation about foreign investment. |
| **Nick**  | Yeah, yeah, it was really interesting. You know, I’m **in a bit of a hurry**, but here’s my card. We should definitely meet up again and continue our discussion.     |
| **Anna**  | Sure. You still have my **contact details**, right?                                                                                                                   |
| **Nick**  | You know what, this is really embarrassing, but your name has just **slipped my mind**. Can you remind me?                                                            |
| **Anna**  | Sure! My name is Anna Ferris. Don’t worry about it, it happens to me all the time. I’m **terrible with names** too.                                                   |
